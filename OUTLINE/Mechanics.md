frame work

combat in the tower is not just fighting, its:
- ritual
- desperation
- corruption
- linguistic violence
its a seperate screen that occurs when the enemy draws near, you can still shoot before they get too close, but that can sometimes be for the worse.

youre not killing things, youre trying to undo their reasons, erase their names,
or unwrite the glitch they emerged from.

Core Components
each enemy has: [these are all things to be added]
- Aspect(s)
	- shame, loop, hunger, judgement, static, echo, stone, etc
- Tension (1 - 6), how unstable it is (like HP)
	- e.g. tension at 3, it multiplies, or at tension 1 it can be bound
- Triggers - what causes it to act
	- sound, movement, scent, thought, etc

Player actions
on each of your turns in this combat minigame you can do an amount of things equal to your speed
- use a verb
- burn a fuel ( resource or memory)
- or just move / wait / observe

VERBS
the players toolbox
each character or item gives access to verbs
examples
VERB 	| Description
REBUKE 	| Counter a hostile idea or command
Forget	| Remove an aspect from memory
Echo	| Reflect a signal or past event
Mend	| Heal or restore
Chant	| Begin a ritual requiring multiple rounds
Feed 	| Offer something to satify or distract
Seal	| Bind or trap something conceptually
Lie	| trick reality into reshaping the encounter

FUEL
what powers the verb

each action costs something
- GLIMMER (weird essence, found littered around the tower)
- MEMORY ( You permanently forget one from your memory)
- Ash (raise your entropy / ash stat
- Flesh (HP)
- Items (e.g. offer RED STRING, Burn CANDLE OF DOUBT etc)
- Turns (multiround innvocation)

Using the wrong fuel may backfire or do nothing. Certain enemies require specific fuel types to be harmed or affected

TARGETS
you can aim at:
- Enemies
- Objects
- Yourself
- The Tower
- Abstracts (a sound, a shadow etc)

COMBAT EXAMPLE
You Encounter:
The ARCHIVIST LOOP
- Aspects - Memory, Loop, Silence
- Tension - 4
- trigger - movement
- Threshold - 2: splits into child processes

You decide to :
- Verb: Forget
- Target: Archivist
- Fuel: Burn a memory: "The Taste of a Childhood Room"

result - You erase its "loop" aspect. It forgets to reset. Tension drops to 2

Next Round

It duplicates
one copy is unstable (new aspect)
you prepare a seal using a fragment of glimmer and your own blood
%chance happens

Success!

one copy is sealed. The other just stares. it begins reciting your memories in reverse order

and so on

ASH AND THE SPIRAL
Every failed invocation, every Fuel burned carelessly, raises your Ash stat

at high ash stat:
- Commands randomly misfire
- Glitch events manifest
- The Towers begins to whisper suggestions
- YOur HUD lies to you
- at 100 ASH your class is restricted to "The HUSK" until you reduce your ash stat back down, or atone for sin
